HL2RP Documentation

Welcome to the documentation for the HL2RP Schema.

The HL2RP Schema is a foundational template designed to support immersive role-playing experiences within the Half-Life 2 universe. It provides essential functionalities and a robust structure that can be customized and expanded to meet the specific needs of your game server.

To utilize this schema, ensure you have the Lilia Framework installed.
